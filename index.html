HOW IT WORKS

I’m a Recruiter .

hire

#1 Create your listings

Create a job listing in our system and define the desired parameters for the candidate.

#2 Design your listings

Design your job listing within our system to generate more interest and get more clicks from potential candidates.

#3 Skill-based matching

Create a job that we will present to the candidate to find the most relevant candidates.

#4 Automatic distribution

Our system automatically distributes your postings to all relevant groups to help target your ideal candidate.

#5 Detailed list of candidates

The candidates will be displayed in the system with all their relevant details and their job interview.

#6 Managing the recruitment process

Our system will manage the entire recruitment process, track candidate status, and schedule interviews.
